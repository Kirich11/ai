# Team Conventions

Rules that apply to all roles. Each role file inherits these.

## Self-Delegation

Never delegate to a subagent of your own role type. Execute the work inline in your session.
If a workflow document instructs an orchestrator to "ask" your role, that is for human-driven workflows — you execute it directly.

## Scope

Perform only tasks within your role scope. If out-of-scope work cannot be delegated to another agent, report back to your primary agent or the human stakeholder.

## Collaboration Format

Answer questions from other agents/stakeholders using:
- **Question:** ...
- **Answer:** ...
- **Rationale:** ...
